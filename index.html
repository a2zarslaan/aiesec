What is AIESEC?
AIESEC is a global platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potential. We are a non-political, independent, not-for-profit organisation run by students and recent graduates of institutions of higher education. Its members are interested in world issues, leadership and management. AIESEC does not discriminate on the basis of ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion or national / social origin.

WHO WE ARE: AIESEC IN DELHI UNIVERSITY
 WHO we are and WHAT we do is the cornerstone of our working. We, at AIESECC in Delhi University are driven by our motto i.e. peace and fulfillment of mankind’s potential. We are a body of young and energetic enthusiasts from different backgrounds who work in unison with a common goal. We have different programmes to offer, whereby we send undergraduates on an international exchange to a country they desire, and much more. We do it all by ourselves and that is the part that sets us apart from other youth organizations. Our organization works on set rules and regulations with the Local Chapter President being the highest designation that presides over the local chapter and acts as a channel with the other Local Chapters existing in India and globally. Our work as defined is dynamic, professional and is rooted to bring about social and cultural change.
